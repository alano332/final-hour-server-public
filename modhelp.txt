/staff. Show online staff.
/modchat or /m <message>. Send a moderator chat.
/tellmod <target> <message>. Sends a tell as a moderator to a player - player should respond with /modtell.
/mute <target> <reason>. Mutes a given player for a given reason.
/unmute <target>. Unmuted the given player.
/kick <target> <public/private> <reason>. Kick the given player. If public, reason is shown to everyone, if private, reason is only shown to staff and kicked player.
/tickets <staff/closed>. Opens the standard tickets menu. Appending staff to the command will show all open tickets and appending closed will show all closed tickets.
/builderhelp. Show a message with builder commands.
